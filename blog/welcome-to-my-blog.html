Welcome To My Blog

July 9, 2026

Hey, welcome to the first post on here. This is going to be a running log of whatever I'm learning at the moment — software, hardware, or the messy overlap between the two. Think debugging notes, small project write-ups, and the occasional "here's a thing I broke and how I fixed it."

I'm still early in this journey, so expect a lot of beginner-level experiments: microcontroller projects, little scripts, circuits that may or may not smoke on the first try. The goal isn't to sound like an expert — it's to actually track progress and have something to look back on later.

More posts coming as I build things worth writing about. Thanks for stopping by.
